My Mission

When I was growing up, My family and I would go from apartment to apartment not knowing when the next move would come. My parents would work long hours to make ends meet but would keep our family afloat through tough times. Eventually, we made enough money to afford the down payment on a house. What my parents did not know was the consequences of taking a loan and who to contact regarding the house. We almost declared bankruptcy. At the same time, my mother got word that her father had passed away and she would come to inherit his 50% of his wealth. Using this money, we bought the house with cash and still own the house to this day!


I became a real estate agent to help families buy their first home and to help them understand the full process of buying a home! I explain everything about owning house. Everything down to the last minute detail because I do not want another family to go through what my family went through!
